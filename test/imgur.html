10 movies with twist endings

by seltic 13h

1. Buried Paul is a U.S. truck driver working in Iraq. After an attack by a group of Iraqis he wakes to find he is buried alive inside a coffin. With only a lighter and a cell phone it's a race against time to escape this claustrophobic death trap. Great movie, made so that you can feel the claustrophobia too.

2. Devil A group of people are trapped in an elevator and the Devil is mysteriously amongst them. Very creepy film, with an ending that makes you rethink through the entire film.

3. Donnie Darko A troubled teenager is plagued by visions of a large bunny rabbit that manipulates him to commit a series of crimes, after narrowly escaping a bizarre accident. You probably wont understand this movie the first time you watch it, or the second time.. I had to look up and explanation to the ending on youtube, and then its like a major mind fuck.

4. Exam The final candidates for a highly desirable corporate job are locked together in an exam room and given a test so simple and confusing that tension begins to unravel. An ending that twists everything around and its like OH ARE YOU FUCKING KIDDING THAT WAS THE ANSWER.

5. Hide and Seek As a widower tries to piece together his life in the wake of his wife's suicide, his daughter finds solace -- at first -- in her imaginary friend. Psychological horror, with another fantastic twisted ending.

6. Orphan A husband and wife who recently lost their baby adopt a 9-year-old girl who is not nearly as innocent as she claims to be. The ending will make you shiver.

8. Shutter Island In 1954, U.S. Marshal Teddy Daniels is investigating the disappearance of a murderess who escaped from a hospital for the criminally insane and is presumed to be hiding near-by.

9. The Sixth Sense A boy who communicates with spirits that don't know they're dead seeks the help of a disheartened child psychologist. Everyone knows this film, mainly because of its fucking incredible twisted ending. If you haven't seen it, watch it!

10. The Prestige The rivalry between two magicians becomes more exacerbated by their attempt to perform the ultimate illusion. Confusing, but amazingly done.
